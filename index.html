Beril's Medical Center

Beril's Medical Center was founded in the early 2020 under the able leadership of its founding Directors; Jonny Brici and Newton Ogada, with the mission and vision of serving the community with quality and affordable healthcare services.

"The facility started its operations with a 10 year old girl named Beril, moved to a small clinic the later we acquired a bigger place, and ever since we have continued to grow."

Newton Ogada, Director

Welcome to Beril's Medical Center

Beril's Medical Center is a modern healthcare facility. Its main objective is to establish and to provide quality and affordable health care services. Its operation began in early 2020 and has since achieved commeandable milestones.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We are committed to giving back to the community in which we operates and thus we have held several health drives through medical camps at the facility and in churches to educate people about dangers, prevention, early detection of diseases such as cancer, diabetes and also to create awareness to the least advantaged in the community. In future we intend on giving free medical talks in government offices regarding chronic illnesses and cancer.
